### Reinstall zsh

1.  sudo rm -r /usr/share/oh-my-zsh
2.  sh -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.github.com/ohmyzsh/ohmyzsh/master/tools/install.sh)"
3.  omz reload

2. download flatpak
3. flatpak install <package>
